## Common Questions

### What is ThreeItemsDaily?

ThreeItemsDaily is a simple daily planner that lets you choose exactly three focus items for the day, track progress, and review history.

### How do I edit my tasks?

Open the app and tap the edit button to update your three daily tasks, notes, or priority order.

### Can I complete more than 3 tasks?

The app is intentionally limited to three main tasks per day. The goal is to reduce overwhelm and help you focus.

### Is my data private?

Your task data stays on your device unless you choose to add cloud syncing in a future version.

### I found a bug. What should I include in my message?

Please include:

- Device model
- iOS version
- App version
- What happened
- Steps to reproduce the issue

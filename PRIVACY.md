# Privacy Policy for ThreeItemsDaily

Effective date: March 13, 2026

ThreeItemsDaily respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

## Overview

ThreeItemsDaily is a simple productivity app that helps you focus on your top three daily tasks. The app is designed to be minimal and privacy-friendly.

## Information We Collect

ThreeItemsDaily stores the task information you enter, such as:

- task titles
- optional notes
- task completion status
- task history created through normal app use

This information is used only so the app can function properly on your device.

## Personal Data

ThreeItemsDaily does not require you to create an account.

ThreeItemsDaily does not collect personal information such as:

- your name
- email address
- phone number
- location
- contacts
- photos
- payment information

## Analytics and Tracking

ThreeItemsDaily does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

ThreeItemsDaily does not track you across apps, websites, or services.

## Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device unless a future version of the app adds optional cloud sync features.

If cloud sync is added in a future update, this Privacy Policy will be updated to explain what data is synced and how it is handled.

## Data Sharing

ThreeItemsDaily does not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

## Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you delete it, remove the app, or clear the app’s stored data.

## Children’s Privacy

ThreeItemsDaily is not directed to children under 13, and the app does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## Security

Reasonable care is taken to keep your data secure on the device, but no method of electronic storage is guaranteed to be completely secure.

## Your Choices

You can stop all collection and storage of data by uninstalling the app from your device.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted in this repository or wherever the app’s support information is published.
